PRIVACY & POLICY


Effective Date: 26/10/2023

1. Introduction Welcome to KindShare, a non-profit organization dedicated to facilitating donations from generous individuals to NGOs in need. Our commitment to your privacy and data security is paramount. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information. By using the KindShare website or services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect 2.1 Personal Information: We may collect personal information, such as your name, email address, contact details, and location when you interact with our website or services. This information is essential for us to facilitate donations and arrange for volunteer transport. 2.2 Donation Information: To coordinate the donation process, we may collect details about the items you wish to donate, such as descriptions, quantities, and condition. This information helps us match donations with NGOs effectively. 2.3 Volunteer Information: If you volunteer as a transporter for donations, we may collect additional information necessary for coordination and safety, including driver's license and vehicle details.

3. How We Use Your Information 3.1 Donation Facilitation: We use your personal information and donation details to connect you with NGOs that can benefit from your generosity. 3.2 Transport Coordination: For volunteers, we use personal and vehicle information to coordinate the collection and delivery of donations securely. 3.3 Communication: We may use your contact information to communicate with you about donation arrangements, updates, and other relevant matters. 3.4 Improvements: We may analyze aggregated, non-identifiable data to enhance our services and user experience.

4. Information Sharing We respect your privacy. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share your information under certain circumstances: With NGOs to ensure efficient donation processes. With volunteers to facilitate donation transportation. With legal authorities in response to a lawful request.

5. Data Security We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, please understand that no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and gather information about website usage. You may configure your browser to reject cookies or notify you when they are being used.

7. Your Choices You have the right to update, correct, or delete your personal information by contacting us. You may opt out of receiving emails from us at any time.

8. Children's Privacy KindShare does not intentionally collect personal information from individuals under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with their personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove the information.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy KindShare may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. You will be notified of any significant changes through our website.

10. Contact Us If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at [Email Address].

Thank you for supporting KindShare in our mission to make the world a kinder place through your generosity and compassion.

At Kind Share, we consider the privacy of our donors, volunteers, and users to be of utmost importance, and we are dedicated to upholding the highest standards of data protection and security. This Privacy Policy serves to comprehensively elucidate our practices concerning the collection, utilization, disclosure, and safeguarding of your personal data. By engaging with our services, you implicitly agree to the terms and conditions detailed within this Privacy Policy.

In our efforts to provide a transparent and accountable approach, we collect various types of personal information from donors and volunteers, encompassing, but not limited to, names, contact information (including email, phone, and address), and, when essential for donation processing and communication, certain financial information. Alongside this, we meticulously maintain records of donations, recording specifics such as the nature and quantity of items donated, donation dates, and the pertinent donor information associated with each contribution. This data plays a pivotal role in enabling us to acknowledge your donations, express our gratitude, offer regular updates on Kind Share's activities, and, with your express consent, disseminate newsletters and promotional materials that aim to keep you informed about our initiatives. Moreover, we use this information for internal analysis and reporting purposes, enabling us to enhance our services, bolster transparency, and better understand donor and volunteer preferences.

In our commitment to maintaining a robust and transparent donation process, Kind Share diligently retains comprehensive records of all donations, ensuring that every item meets the requisite quality and safety standards. These records are indispensable not only for the sake of accurate tracking but also for issuing donation acknowledgments and tax receipts where applicable. Rest assured that the security of your data is paramount, and we employ industry-standard security measures, encompassing encryption, secure data storage, access controls, and periodic security assessments. Nonetheless, it's essential to acknowledge that while we take stringent precautions, no method of data transmission or storage can be entirely invulnerable, and we cannot offer an absolute guarantee against unauthorized access or data breaches.

Regarding the sharing of data, Kind Share may, when necessary, share limited donor information with our partnering NGOs to facilitate the distribution process, ensuring that donated items reach the intended beneficiaries. We also engage third-party service providers for functions such as website maintenance, communication tools, or payment processing, and these providers may have access to certain personal information. However, these providers are contractually bound to maintain the confidentiality and security of this data. Additionally, we may disclose personal information if required by law or in response to lawful requests from public authorities, particularly to meet tax reporting and compliance obligations.

We respect your rights concerning your personal data. You have the prerogative to access, correct, or delete your personal information. For inquiries about your data or to exercise these rights, please reach out to us through the contact information provided. Kind Share may update this Privacy Policy periodically to align with changes in our practices or applicable regulations. These updates will be promptly posted on our website, and any revisions will be effective immediately. For any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy, Kind Share's services, or your personal data, do not hesitate to contact us at the provided contact information. Your privacy is paramount, and your trust is fundamental as we work collectively to make a positive impact on the lives of those in need.

We extend our heartfelt gratitude for your support and collaboration in making the world a better place for those who need it most. Your compassion and generosity truly make a difference in the lives of many. Together, we can continue to bring kindness and support to the world's most vulnerable populations.
